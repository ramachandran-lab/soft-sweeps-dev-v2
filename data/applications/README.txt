UCSC hg19 Known Gene track was downloaded from UCSC Table Browser on 11/27/2017: Mammal > Human > Feb. 2009 using following steps: (GRCh37/hg19) > Genes and Gene Predictions > UCSC Genes > knownGene > genome > BED - browser extensible data > hg19-ucsc_genes-known_gene.bed > gzip compressed > get output > Whole Gene > get BED; Mammal > Human > Feb. 2009 (GRCh37/hg19) > Genes and Gene Predictions > UCSC Genes > knownGene > genome > output format: BED - browser extensible data > hg19-ucsc_genes-known_gene-exons.bed > gzip compressed > get output > Exons plus > get BED; Mammal > Human > Feb. 2009 (GRCh37/hg19) > Genes and Gene Predictions > UCSC Genes > knownGene > genome > selected fields from primary and related tables > hg19-ucsc_genes-known_gene-names.txt > gzip compressed > get output > name + chrom + strand + exonCount + geneSymbol + description > get output.
